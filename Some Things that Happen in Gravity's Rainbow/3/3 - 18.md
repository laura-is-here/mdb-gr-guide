[18], 488-491. As a storm batters the _Anubis_, Slothrop searches for Bianca. He is beginning to suspect that the "Schwarzgerät" is not a clue, but bait, manipulated by "Them." And he is changing: "He is growing less anxious about betraying those who trust him." (490) Thinking he sees Bianca going overboard, he lunges--and goes overboard himself.
