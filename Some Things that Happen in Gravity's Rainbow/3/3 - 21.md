[21], 519-525. Enzian and two Herero subordinates, Andreas and Christian, burst into an abandoned room. They are too late; Christian's sister, Maria, has already been abducted by Joseph Ombindi and the Empty Ones (see [3: \[3\]](3%20-%203.md), above), for an enforced abortion. As Enzian and his cohorts head for the (Jamf) refinery on motorcycles, Enzian begins to see the bombing of factories not as random destruction but as deliberate _modification_--"part of a plan both sides--'_sides_?'--had always agreed on." (520) The true War, he now speculates, was "dictated . . . by the needs of technology." (521; cf. [105](1%20-%2014.md), above?) Another song, "Just a daredevil Desox-yephedine Daddy" (522) At the refinery the motorcycle trio finds Pavel, Maria's husband, hallucinating, high from sniffing Leunagasolin. Again, Enzian thinks of his mission: to find the center (whether rocket, refinery, Volkswagen factory), the "true text." He fantasizes an address to all the assembled Erdschweinhöhle (see [1: \[3\]](1%20-%203.md), above): "_My people, I have had a vision_" (525; cf. M.L. King, Jr.?). "Somewhere," he thinks, formulating the ideology of his "new Search," ". . . is the key that will bring us back, restore us to our Earth and to our freedom." (525) From Pavel, finally, the three Hereros get the address of "Ombindi's medical connection."
