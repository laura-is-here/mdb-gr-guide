[15], 468-472. Slothrop dreams about Llandudno, "where Lewis Carroll wrote that _Alice in Wonderland_." Bianca appears at his bedside, ready and willing. After sex, she asks him to escape with her: "I'm a child, I know how to hide. I can hide you too." (470) Slothrop agrees; but as he leaves (in "a bureaucracy of departure") "coming back is something he's already forgotten about." (470-71) Her look "has already broken Slothrop's seeing heart," but he _does_ leave.
