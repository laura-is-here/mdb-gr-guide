[4], 329-336. lothrop and Geli are on the top of the Brocken, awaiting sunrise. We learn about Slothrop's Antinomian-witch ancestor, Amy Sprue, hanged at Salem (329). Slothrop and Geli, as the sun rises, watch their gigantic shadows in the clouds. Since Marvy is still after him, Slothrop heads for Berlin (by Geli's arrangement), in a balloon, with one Schnorp--who's flying in custard pies to sell on the black market. They are pursued by Marvy and his Mothers in a plane. More rocket limericks (334-5). The balloon, after a custard-pie battle, escapes.
