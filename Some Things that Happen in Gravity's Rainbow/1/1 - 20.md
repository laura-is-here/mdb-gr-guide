[20], 167-174. The PISCES Christmas party. Pointsman and Maude Chilkes in the closet. Pointsman and Gwenhidwy go to the latter's flat. Gwenhidwy to Pointsman (on what we will learn to call the "preterite"): "How they _persist_." East London as the "City Paranoiac": Gwenhidwy imagines (or perceives?) that the poor and outcast have been _placed_ here to absorb the brunt of rocket attacks from the Southeast.
